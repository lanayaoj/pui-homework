¡Bienvenidos!

I’m Lizeth Anaya-Ojeda, an aspiring user experience designer committed to creating inclusive technology solutions.

Why Statement

My purpose is to bridge the digital divide by creating technology that is accessible to everyone, especially marginalized communities. My journey as an undocumented immigrant has fueled my desire to advocate for those who often lack representation in tech development.

Values

Empathy and compassion guide my work. I believe in the power of collaboration and the importance of listening to diverse voices in the design process. My experiences have taught me that understanding the needs of others is key to creating meaningful solutions.

Future Aspirations

Looking ahead, I aim to leverage my skills in Human-Computer Interaction to develop intuitive interfaces for immigrant populations and advocate for ethical considerations in technology. I envision a future where technology transcends barriers and empowers diverse communities to thrive.
